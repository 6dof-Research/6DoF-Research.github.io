6DoF Research is an Immersive Experience Lab specializing in the technologies that drive live spatial events. Whether it is VR, AR, or sensor-driven physical installations, we strive to create dynamic, interactive, experiences that blend physical and digital realities.

We value commodity-solutions, open-source, interoperability, accessibility, and privacy in everything we make. We believe embracing these values maximizes value for our clients while reaching the greatest possible audience.

We offer expertise for every stage of making a truly unique immersive experience including creative, production, distribution, analytics and publishing.
